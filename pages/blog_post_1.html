“Impact of technology on the watch industry and consumer trends”

By Jayant Subedi January 15, 2025

Blog 1 Image

Technology has deeply transformed the watch industry by changing customer preferences and revolutionizing the way watches are designed and marketed.

In the present day, the rise of smartwatches like Apple and Android-based watches has restructured the market. These watches provide multifunctional features such as fitness tracking, heart rate monitoring, and mobile phone connectivity.
On the other hand, hybrid watches offer traditional analog designs combined with smart features, attracting people who desire a mix of classic appearance and modern functionality.

Furthermore, the use of automation and AI in manufacturing has made watch production more efficient. At the same time, increased interest in sustainability has led to the adoption of eco-friendly materials and solar-powered models.

Nowadays, customers are also showing greater interest in personalized and customizable watches. As technology continues to advance, the watch industry is increasingly focused on offering products that interconnect style, functionality, and environmental responsibility to attract modern customers.
