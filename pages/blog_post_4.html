“Is Technology Taking Away the Traditional Watchmaking Art?”

By Dipti Subedi January 30, 2025

Blog 4 Image

In this 21st century, watch making companies are growing up with their new innovation of chronograph which is not only durable also lightweight. In order to achieve the perfect balance, there must be understanding of materials, engineering, and other technologies. Materials like titanium and ceramic, which are lightweight yet durable, are used to make watch which doesn't even feel heavy.

Along with titanium and ceramic, carbon fiber also plays an important role in making watches very light yet strong which might be a little expensive. Also, for watch's strength and weight, strap material is also considered as another factor. Selecting a lighter strap material can further reduce the overall weight of the watch.
